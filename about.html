Our Story

Founded in 2023, Xeno Interactions LLC emerged from a passion for creating immersive virtual reality experiences. Based in Indiana, USA, we're a dedicated team of developers and visionaries committed to crafting worlds that inspire and engage.

Our flagship project, Tropik, represents our vision of what VR gaming can become - a space where physics-driven gameplay meets social interaction in beautifully crafted virtual worlds.
